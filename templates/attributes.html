Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 200 grams sugar
  • 100 grams butter
  • 150 grams flour
  • 2 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 100 ml milk

Instructions

  1. First take an egg and beat it
  2. then mix all the ingredients together and create a batter
  3. preheat the oven at 250 degrees
  4. put the cake in and let it bake
  5. viola!your delicious cake is ready
